Before you surrender, please know that help is available. We want to explore every option to keep your pet safe and in a loving home.

Resources available to you

We understand that life situations change. Before surrendering your pet, consider these resources that may help you keep your family together.

1 Temporary boarding referrals — short-term housing when you need time to get back on your feet
2 Low-cost vet care resources — affordable medical treatment so cost doesn't force a surrender
3 Behavior support & training referrals — professional help for pets with behavioral challenges
4 Rehoming tools — platforms like Adopt-a-Pet Rehome and local groups to find your pet a new home directly
5 Short-term crisis support — emergency assistance to help you through a difficult period
6 Pet food assistance — free food and supplies to ease financial burden

Need help with any of these? Contact us at Admin@kittypuppy.org or call (305) 902-6467 and we'll help connect you with the right resources.

About our intake process

Most of the pets in our care come through partner referrals, social service agencies, or transfers from other shelters and rescues. Because we are a small, foster-based organization, we rarely have the capacity to accept owner surrenders from the general public.

Submitting a form does not guarantee intake. We review each case individually and will contact you if we're able to help.

We may consider:

  • Pets referred by partner agencies
  • Pets in immediate crisis situations
  • Special-needs cases with an available foster match
