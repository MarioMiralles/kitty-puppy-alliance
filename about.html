Our Story

Compassion for animals & the people who care for them

We were founded on a simple belief: every animal deserves dignity, and every person who loves them deserves support.

How it began

Kitty Puppy Alliance was established by founders who spent years volunteering with animals in Miami's shelters and rescue networks. After witnessing both the incredible need and the gaps in the system, they built an organization centered on compassion — for both animals and the people who care for them.

Today, we operate as a foster-based rescue, taking in cats, dogs, and rabbits from partner referrals, social service agencies, and transfers from other shelters. Every animal in our care receives full veterinary treatment, love, and the time they need to find their perfect match.

The animals who inspire us

Every life we touch shapes who we are. These two souls are at the heart of everything we do.

Misu

A senior Siamese rescued from Miami-Dade Animal Services — deaf, partially blind, and bearing the weight of years of neglect. Despite everything, Misu experienced love and dignity in her final days with us. She inspired our practice of accepting hospice cases, because every animal deserves to know kindness before the end.

Oscar

A young tabby kitten lost to Feline Infectious Peritonitis (FIP). Oscar's short life drives our commitment to never accept "no chance" as an answer. He reminds us that every moment of care matters — and that we fight for every animal, no matter the odds.

Who we are

Zeta

Founder

Zeta began volunteering as a foster parent in 2020. Since then, she has fostered over 400 animals while advocating for animal welfare, disability rights, and housing and homelessness issues. With a personal focus on neonate kitten care, Zeta built Kitty Puppy Alliance around everything she wished existed in rescue: real support, transparency, and a voice for every foster in the process.
